## üèÜ Understanding Your Scores (The 6-Score System)

After every answer, you get immediate feedback. We don't just give you a "Pass/Fail"; we break it down into **6 dimensions**:

### 1. Content (30%)
*   **What it is:** How well did you actually answer the question?
*   **How it works:** We use semantic similarity to compare your answer against an "Ideal Answer" generated for that specific question.
*   **Tip:** Be specific and directly address the prompt.

### 2. Delivery (15%)
*   **What it is:** Your speaking pace and fluency.
*   **How it works:** We measure Words Per Minute (WPM).
    *   *Too Slow (<100 WPM):* Can sound unsure or unprepared.
    *   *Too Fast (>180 WPM):* Can sound nervous or rushed.
    *   *Optimal (130-150 WPM):* Clear and confident.

### 3. Communication (15%)
*   **What it is:** Your command of the language and vocabulary.
*   **How it works:** We detect grammar errors and "Filler Words" (um, ah, like, you know).
*   **Tip:** excessively using "um" will lower this score. Pause instead of using a filler word.

### 4. Voice (15%)
*   **What it is:** Energy and tone consistency.
*   **How it works:** Analyzes the audio signal for variations in pitch and volume to detect monotony vs. engagement.

### 5. Confidence (15%)
*   **What it is:** How assertive you sound.
*   **How it works:** Looks for "weak" language (e.g., "I think", "maybe", "sort of") vs. "strong" language (e.g., "I led", "I achieved", "definitely").

### 6. Structure (10%)
*   **What it is:** Logical flow of your story.
*   **How it works:** Specifically looks for the **STAR Method** components:
    *   **S**ituation: Setting the scene.
    *   **T**ask: What was the goal?
    *   **A**ction: What did *you* do?
    *   **R**esult: What was the outcome?

## ‚ùì Troubleshooting

*   **Microphone Not Working:** Ensure you have granted browser permissions. If using Chrome, check the "Lock" icon in the address bar.
*   **No Feedback:** If the analysis hangs, check your internet connection. Large audio files on slow connections may take longer to upload.
*   **"Quota Exceeded":** The system uses AI APIs that have daily limits. If you see this, try again later or contact the admin.
